About Me

I am a Data Science enthusiast who loves problem solving. Currently, I am associated with [Whatfix](https://www.linkedin.com/company/whatfix) where I am involved in developing solutions that will help the end users of Whatfix use the product in best possible way. I have hands on experience in R, Python, MS-Sql, Excel and SAS but recently, I am mostly working on python.

Ever since I got my first job in the field of data science, I have been at it solving business problems across domains. I believe in the potential of data to empower people and take their best decision. Data Science is a field where every new problem brings its own set of challenges and I often find myself completely engrossed in the problem statement to come up with the best implementable solution possible.

My passion for learning never stops and I am always looking for new ways to solve things and help people. I honestly believe nothing is out of one's reach if we set our mind to it.

I aspire to continue learning and unravel the mysteries surrounding the world of Data Science. I will be using My Blog for the same purpose. I will try to keep things as simple as possible. Looking forward to feedback and comments.
